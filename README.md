## Task 1: Superstore Retail Analysis
**Objective:** Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, and regional performance to provide actionable recommendations.

### Key Insights:
* **Revenue Drivers:** The Technology category generated the highest revenue share (36.4%), while Office Supplies garnered the most unit sales.
* **Regional Disparities:** The West region outperformed the South region by nearly 2x in total sales.
* **Recommendation:** Implement targeted sales, new pricing models & strategies, and regional marketing, aimed at driving up sales & revenue.
